Laser Plates

Product Description: We make Laser Plates available in various sizes and thicknesses to suit your specific needs. Our laser plates are known for their precision, durability, and versatility, making them ideal for a wide range of applications including construction, manufacturing, and artistic projects. We charge per sheet of 4ft by 8ft; and we can accommodate custom sizes upon request. 1 sheet = N75,000 for 1.5mm thickness. This is for Plate, Design and Cutting. We can use other thicknesses as well, depending on your needs. You can make part payments before we start designing and cutting and pay the balance upon delivery. You can make inquiries for bulk orders or specific requirements.

Features:

  • High precision cutting
  • Durable and long-lasting
  • Versatile applications
  • Customizable options
